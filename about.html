Mission Statement

﹌

Eat Wise is a web application intended to empower young professionals in making informed decisions towards achieving a healthier diet. We have more choices and more temptations than ever before. As fast-food chains continually find ways to exploit the human cravings for fats and sweets, Eat-Wise is dedicated to equip consumers with tools and strategies in taking charge of their choices.

Eat Wise app features the ability to populate a list of recipes for the user to make at home, and also to populate a list of restaurant choices in your city. The significance of the generated lists is that they are based on custom user preferences in the categories of cuisine, intolerances, dietary restrictions and meal time. Users are given accurate, precise and real-time information to achieve a more wholesome diet. Future capabilities to give users further customization and guidance can be easily implemented due to the modular programming in our app design.

Users are given accurate, precise and real-time information to achieve a more wholesome diet.

The MVP of Eat Wise provides tremendous value as a tool to simplify the decision making process for choosing what to eat. Our filtering process effortlessly guides you to quickly narrow down your choices. The results generated are detailed with ingredients lists and full instructions for recipes while restaurant search results provide information for rating, location and cuisine.
